1. VACATION POLICY
===================

Annual Vacation Entitlement:

SWEDEN EMPLOYEES:
- All full-time employees in Sweden receive 25 vacation days per year
- Vacation days accrue monthly at a rate of 2.08 days per month
- Employees must take at least 4 consecutive weeks during the summer period (June-August)
- Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year (max 5 days)
- Part-time employees receive pro-rated vacation days based on their working hours

GERMANY EMPLOYEES:
- All full-time employees in Germany receive 30 vacation days per year
- Vacation days accrue monthly at a rate of 2.5 days per month
- No mandatory summer vacation period required
- Unused vacation days expire on March 31st of the following year
- Part-time employees receive pro-rated vacation days based on their working hours

OTHER LOCATIONS:
- Employees in other locations follow local labor law requirements
- Minimum of 20 vacation days per year for all full-time employees
- Consult with HR for specific country requirements

Requesting Vacation:
- Submit vacation requests through the company portal at least 2 weeks in advance
- Manager approval required for all vacation requests
- Peak business periods (November-December) may have restricted vacation availability


===================
2. SICK LEAVE POLICY
===================

Sick Days:
- All employees receive 10 paid sick days per year
- Unused sick days do not carry over to the next year
- Medical certificate required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days

Short-Term Illness (1-5 days):
- Notify your manager within 2 hours of your scheduled start time
- No medical documentation required for up to 3 days
- Use company sick leave system to log absence

Long-Term Illness (6+ days):
- Medical certificate required from day 6 onwards
- Submit certificate to HR within 7 days of absence
- Company provides 100% salary continuation for up to 6 weeks
- After 6 weeks, statutory sick pay applies (varies by country)

Return to Work:
- Coordinate with your manager before returning from extended sick leave
- Medical clearance may be required for certain types of illness


===================
3. REMOTE WORK POLICY
===================

Eligibility:
- Available to all employees whose roles permit remote work
- Manager approval required
- Probationary employees (first 3 months) must work from office

Remote Work Options:
- Full-time remote: Work from home 5 days per week
- Hybrid: Work from home 2-3 days per week, office 2-3 days
- Flexible: Ad-hoc remote work as needed with manager approval

Requirements:
- Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Available during core business hours (9:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time)
- Attend all mandatory in-person meetings and team events

Equipment:
- Company provides laptop and necessary software
- Employees may request monitor, keyboard, mouse (one-time provision)
- Home office stipend: €50 per month for full-time remote workers

Communication:
- Respond to emails within 4 business hours
- Available on company chat platform during working hours
- Video on for all team meetings

4. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
===================

Health Insurance:
- Company provides comprehensive health insurance for all full-time employees
- Coverage begins on first day of employment
- Family coverage available with employee contribution
- Dental and vision coverage included in premium plan

Retirement Benefits:
- Company contributes 5% of base salary to retirement plan
- Employee can contribute additional amount (pre-tax)
- Matching contribution up to 3% of employee contribution
- Vesting period: 2 years

Professional Development:
- Annual learning budget: €1,500 per employee
- Can be used for courses, certifications, conferences
- Manager approval required
- Submit receipts to HR for reimbursement within 60 days

Wellness Programs:
- Gym membership reimbursement: up to €50 per month
- Mental health support: 6 free counseling sessions per year
- Annual health screening provided at company expense
- Wellness days: 2 additional paid days per year for wellness activities

Life Insurance:
- Company provides life insurance coverage equal to 2x annual salary
- Additional coverage available for purchase
- Beneficiary designation required within first 30 days


===================
5. EXPENSE POLICY
===================

Business Expenses:
- All business-related expenses must be pre-approved by manager
- Submit expense reports within 30 days of expense date
- Receipts required for all expenses over €25
- Late submissions may be denied

Travel Expenses:

Flight Bookings:
- Economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class for flights over 6 hours
- Book through company travel portal for best rates
- Last-minute bookings require VP approval

Accommodation:
- Maximum €150 per night for hotels in major cities
- Maximum €100 per night for hotels in other locations
- Book through company travel portal when possible
- Extended stays (5+ nights) may qualify for apartment rental

Meals and Entertainment:
- Daily meal allowance: €50 per day when traveling
- Client entertainment: up to €100 per person with manager approval
- Alcohol: maximum 2 drinks per person, must include food
- Tips: standard local percentage (15-20%)

Ground Transportation:
- Public transportation preferred
- Taxi/ride-share: reasonable cost based on distance
- Car rental: compact or mid-size only, requires manager approval
- Personal vehicle: reimbursement at €0.35 per kilometer

Office Supplies:
- Order through company procurement system
- Personal items (coffee, snacks) not reimbursable
- Technology purchases require IT approval
- Ergonomic equipment approved on case-by-case basis
